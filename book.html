Pizza

by Orlando Mendiola

May 6, 2016

Pizza, you could say, is a widely loved food. Pizza in New York City, takes on a whole new culture with dollar pizza. But what happens when you favorite Dollar pizza place increases their prices? For my Reporting Without Words project, we had to create an 8-part story using strictly images and no words. I hand carved eight 4X6 linoleum blocks to create my story and the finished product is below in a video and in a poster I created of my project.
